**Strategic Advice**

Given that it's early in the game and there are no enemy structures yet, our priority should be to establish a strong economy and military presence. We need to focus on building essential structures, training basic infantry units, and collecting Tiberium.

1. **Economy**: Build multiple Refineries to increase Tiberium income.
2. **Military Presence**: Train basic infantry units (Minigunner) to defend our base and start scouting the map for enemy positions.
3. **Base Expansion**: Construct essential buildings like Barracks, War Factory, and Communications Center.
