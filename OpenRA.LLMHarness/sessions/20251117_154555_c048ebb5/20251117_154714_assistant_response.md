**Strategic Advice**

Based on the current game state, it appears that Jason (GDI) is in a good position with multiple Power Plants and a Construction Yard nearing completion. However, there are no visible units or defensive structures to protect the base from potential Nod attacks.

To secure the base and prepare for an attack, I recommend:

* Building additional defensive structures such as Guard Towers and Gun Turrets around the Construction Yard and Power Plants.
* Training infantry units (Minigunners) to defend the base.
* Constructing a Barracks to train more infantry units and potentially other types of units.
