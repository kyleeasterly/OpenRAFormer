### Strategic Advice

- **Economy Expansion**: 
  - Complete the Refinery currently under construction. This will provide a steady income from Tiberium, which is crucial for sustaining military operations.
  - Consider building additional Refineries once more Tiberium fields are accessible to maximize resource collection.

- **Military Preparation**:
  - With sufficient power and resources, prioritize constructing a Barracks or Hand of Nod. This will allow the production of infantry units necessary for both defense and offense.
  - Plan for a War Factory (GDI) or Airfield (Nod) next to diversify unit capabilities with vehicles and aircraft.

- **Base Defense**:
  - Begin building defensive structures like Guard Towers or Gun Turrets once the Barracks is operational. This will help protect against enemy incursions, especially from Player2's Nod forces.

- **Tech Advancement**:
  - Aim to build a Communications Center after establishing a stable economy and initial military presence. This will unlock advanced units and strategic options like airstrikes.
