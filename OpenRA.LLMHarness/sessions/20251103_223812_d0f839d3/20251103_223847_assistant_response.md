### Strategic Advice

- **Economy Expansion**: 
  - Complete the Power Plant under construction to increase power capacity.
  - Prioritize completing the Refinery, which is already in progress. This will enable Harvester deployment for Tiberium collection and income generation.

- **Military Preparation**:
  - Once economic infrastructure (Power Plant and Refinery) is stable, focus on building a Barracks or Hand of Nod to start training infantry units.
  - Consider constructing a War Factory or Airfield next to diversify military capabilities with vehicles or aircraft.

- **Base Defense**:
  - Plan for defensive structures like Guard Towers or Gun Turrets once the economy allows. This will help protect against early enemy attacks, especially from Player cabal (Nod).

- **Map Control and Exploration**:
  - Use Harvester units to explore and secure additional Tiberium fields.
  - Deploy a few infantry units for reconnaissance and potential harassment of enemy structures.
