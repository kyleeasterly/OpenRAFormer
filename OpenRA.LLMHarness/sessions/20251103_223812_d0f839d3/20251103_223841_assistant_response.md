### Strategic Advice

- **Economy Development**: With a Power Plant ready, prioritize constructing a Refinery next. This will enable Harvester deployment for Tiberium collection, crucial for economic growth.
  
- **Military Preparation**: Once the economy is stable with multiple refineries and harvesters, begin building military infrastructure like Barracks or War Factory to train units.

- **Map Control**: Utilize the visibility advantage from the "Explored Map" setting to scout enemy positions and plan strategic expansions or attacks.

- **Tech Advancement**: Plan for future tech upgrades by saving resources for a Communications Center, which will unlock advanced units and defenses.
