### Strategic Advice

- **Economy Development**: 
  - With $7000 in cash and a Power Plant ready, prioritize constructing a Refinery. This will enable Harvester production for Tiberium collection.
  - Consider building multiple Refineries once the first one is operational to maximize resource income.

- **Military Preparation**:
  - Once the economy is stable with active Tiberium harvesting, start producing military units from Barracks or Hand of Nod (depending on faction).
  - Focus on a balanced force: infantry for defense and support, vehicles for offense and control.

- **Infrastructure Expansion**:
  - After establishing a Refinery, build a Barracks to train basic infantry units.
  - Plan for future expansion by constructing a War Factory or Airfield next, depending on strategic needs (vehicles vs. air superiority).

- **Defensive Measures**:
  - Consider building defensive structures like Guard Towers or Gun Turrets once the economy allows, especially near resource-rich areas.
