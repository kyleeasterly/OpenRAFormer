### Strategic Advice

- **Economy Development**: 
  - Ensure continuous Tiberium harvesting by constructing additional Refineries once more Tiberium fields are discovered. This will increase resource income, allowing for faster unit and building production.
  
- **Military Preparation**:
  - Begin training basic infantry units like Minigunners from the Barracks to defend against early aggression and secure your base.
  - Consider starting a War Factory or Airfield soon after completing the Refinery to diversify military capabilities with vehicles and aircraft.

- **Infrastructure Expansion**:
  - Complete the Power Plant to increase power capacity, enabling more buildings and units to be produced simultaneously.
  - Once the Barracks is complete, start producing Minigunners for base defense. 

- **Base Defense**:
  - Construct defensive structures like Guard Towers or Gun Turrets once the Barracks is operational to protect against enemy infantry and vehicles.
