### Strategic Advice

- **Deploy MCV**: The Mobile Construction Vehicle is already deployed. Ensure it's in a safe location near resources for constructing the first buildings.
  
- **Build Power Plant**: Start by building a Power Plant to provide power, which is essential for further construction and unit production.

- **Secure Resources**: Identify nearby Tiberium fields to plan future Refinery placements, ensuring efficient resource collection.

- **Expand Economy**: Once the Power Plant is operational, build a Refinery on a Tiberium field. This will allow Harvester deployment for resource gathering.

- **Base Defense**: Consider building basic defensive structures like Sandbags or Chain Link Fences as soon as possible to protect your base from early attacks.

- **Military Preparation**: After establishing an economy, start producing basic infantry units (Minigunners) and vehicles (Humvees) for defense and potential aggression.
