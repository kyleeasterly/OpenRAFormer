=== Command & Conquer Strategy Guide for OpenRA ===

This guide explains the basic mechanics and units for the C&C mod in OpenRA.
GDI and Nod share most basic units with faction-specific advanced units.

== BUILD ORDER REQUIREMENTS ==

You MUST follow this exact order at game start:
1. Deploy your MCV (Mobile Construction Vehicle) to create your Construction Yard
2. Build a Power Plant - Cost: $500, Provides: 100 power
3. Build a Refinery - Cost: $1500, Requires: Power Plant, Includes: 1 free Harvester
4. Build Barracks - Cost: $500, Requires: Power Plant
5. Build War Factory (GDI) or Airfield (Nod) - Cost: $2000, Requires: Refinery
6. Build Communications Center - Cost: $1000, Requires: Refinery (enables advanced units)

== ECONOMY ==

- Tiberium is your only resource, appearing as green or blue crystals on the map
- Harvesters automatically collect Tiberium and deliver to Refineries
- Each Refinery comes with 1 free Harvester when built
- Additional Harvesters cost $1100 and require War Factory/Airfield
- Build multiple Refineries on different Tiberium fields for faster income
- Tiberium Storage Silos ($100) increase storage capacity, Requires: Refinery

== KEY STRUCTURES ==

=== Basic Buildings ===
- Construction Yard: Your command center, build from deployed MCV
- Power Plant: $500, provides 100 power, Requires: Construction Yard
- Advanced Power Plant: $800, provides 200 power, Requires: Communications Center
- Refinery: $1500, processes Tiberium, includes 1 Harvester, Requires: Power Plant
- Barracks: $500, trains infantry, Requires: Power Plant
- War Factory (GDI): $2000, builds vehicles, Requires: Refinery
- Airfield (Nod): $2000, builds vehicles via airdrop, Requires: Refinery

=== Defensive Structures ===
- Guard Tower (GDI): $600, Anti-infantry defense, Requires: Barracks
- Turret (Nod): $600, Anti-vehicle defense, Requires: Barracks
- SAM Site: $650, Anti-air defense, Requires: Hand of Nod
- Obelisk of Light (Nod): $1500, Advanced laser defense, Requires: Temple of Nod

=== Advanced Buildings ===
- Communications Center: $1000, Provides radar minimap, Requires: Refinery
- Temple of Nod: $2000, Nod tech center, enables Nuclear Missile, Requires: Communications Center
- Advanced Communications Center: $1800, GDI tech center, enables Ion Cannon, Requires: Communications Center
- Helipad: $1000, Builds and rearms aircraft, Requires: Refinery
- Repair Facility: $500, Repairs vehicles, Requires: War Factory or Airfield
- Silo: $100, Stores extra Tiberium, Requires: Refinery

== INFANTRY UNITS ==

- Minigunner: $100, basic anti-infantry, Requires: Barracks
- Grenadier: $160, anti-infantry/building (GDI), Requires: Communications Center
- Rocket Soldier: $300, anti-tank/air, Requires: Barracks
- Flamethrower Infantry: $200, anti-infantry/building (Nod), Requires: Communications Center
- Chemical Warrior: $300, advanced anti-infantry (Nod), Requires: Temple of Nod
- Engineer: $500, captures buildings, repairs bridges, Requires: Barracks
- Commando: $1500, sniper + C4 demolitions (GDI), Requires: Advanced Communications Center

== VEHICLES ==

=== Support ===
- Harvester: $1100, collects Tiberium (first one free with Refinery), Requires: Refinery
- Mobile Construction Vehicle (MCV): $3000, deploys into Construction Yard, Requires: War Factory/Airfield
- Armored Personnel Carrier (APC): $600, transports 5 infantry (GDI), Requires: Barracks

=== Combat Vehicles ===
- Nod Buggy: $300, fast scout with machine gun, Requires: Airfield
- Recon Bike: $500, fast rocket scout, Requires: Airfield
- Humvee: $400, light vehicle (GDI), Requires: War Factory
- Light Tank: $750, basic tank (Nod), Requires: Communications Center
- Medium Tank: $900, main battle tank (GDI), Requires: Communications Center
- Flame Tank: $600, anti-infantry (Nod), Requires: Communications Center
- Artillery: $600, long-range support (Nod), Requires: Communications Center
- Mammoth Tank: $1800, heavy dual-cannon tank (GDI), Requires: Advanced Communications Center
- Stealth Tank: $900, cloaked missile tank (Nod), Requires: Temple of Nod
- Rocket Launcher: $900, mobile SAM (GDI), Requires: Communications Center
- Mobile Rocket Launch System (MLRS): $600, anti-air (Nod), Requires: Communications Center

== AIRCRAFT ==

- Orca VTOL: $1200, gunship with missiles (GDI), Requires: Helipad and Communications Center
- Apache Attack Helicopter: $1200, gunship (Nod), Requires: Helipad and Communications Center
- Chinook Transport: $750, transport helicopter, Requires: Helipad

== STRATEGY TIPS ==

1. Economy First: Build order is critical - Power → Refinery → Production
2. Multiple Refineries: Better than multiple Harvesters early game
3. Scout with cheap units: Use Minigunners or Buggies, NOT transports
4. Combined Arms: Mix infantry, vehicles, and air units
5. Engineers: Can capture enemy buildings for tech stealing
6. Power Management: Buildings operate slowly without sufficient power
7. Base Defense: Guard Towers vs infantry, Turrets vs vehicles
8. Superweapons: Ion Cannon (GDI) and Nuclear Missile (Nod) require tech centers
