# Command & Conquer Strategy Guide

This guide explains the basic mechanics and units for Command & Conquer.
GDI and Nod share most basic units with faction-specific advanced units.

# Build Order
You MUST follow this exact order at game start:
1. Deploy your MCV (Mobile Construction Vehicle) to create your Construction Yard
2. Build a Power Plant - Cost: $500, Provides: 100 power
3. Build a Refinery - Cost: $1500, Requires: Power Plant, Includes: 1 free Harvester
4. Build Barracks - Cost: $500, Requires: Power Plant
5. Build Weapons Factory (GDI) or Airfield (Nod) - Cost: $2000, Requires: Refinery
6. Build Communications Center - Cost: $1000, Requires: Refinery (enables advanced units)

# Economy
- Tiberium is your only resource, appearing as green or blue crystals on the map
- Harvesters automatically collect Tiberium and deliver to Refineries
- Each Refinery comes with 1 free Harvester when built
- Build multiple Refineries on different Tiberium fields for faster income
- Tiberium Storage Silos ($100) increase storage capacity, but try to avoid building them and efficiently turn your income into units instead

# Strategy Tips
1. Economy First: Build order is critical - Power → Refinery → Production
2. Multiple Refineries: One refinery per Tiberium field for max income
4. Combined Arms: Mix infantry, vehicles, and air units
6. Power Management: Buildings operate slowly without sufficient power
7. Base Defense: Guard Towers vs infantry, Turrets vs vehicles
8. Superweapons: Ion Cannon (GDI) and Nuclear Missile (Nod) require tech centers

## Basic Buildings
- Construction Yard: $3000, 01:12, 0 Power, Base building deployed from MCV
- Power Plant: $500, 00:12, +100 Power, Requires: Construction Yard
- Advanced Power Plant: $800, 00:19, +200 Power, Requires: Communications Center
- Refinery: $1500, 00:36, -40 Power, Requires: Power Plant, Processes Tiberium, includes 1 free Harvester
- Barracks (GDI): $500, 00:12, -15 Power, Requires: Power Plant, Trains infantry
- Hand of Nod: $500, 00:12, -15 Power, Requires: Power Plant, Trains infantry  
- War Factory (GDI): $2000, 00:48, -40 Power, Requires: Refinery, Builds vehicles
- Airfield (Nod): $2000, 00:48, -40 Power, Requires: Refinery, Airdrops vehicles

## Defensive Structures

- Chain Link Fence (Nod): $25, 00:01, 0 Power, Requires: Construction Yard, Basic wall

- Guard Tower: $600, 00:23, -10 Power, Requires: Barracks, Anti-infantry defense
- Gun Turret: $600, 00:23, -20 Power, Requires: Barracks, Anti-vehicle defense

- Advanced Guard Tower (GDI): $1000, 00:46, -50 Power, Requires: Communications Center, All-purpose defense, Strong vs Aircraft and Infantry
- Obelisk of Light (Nod): $1500, 00:50, -90 Power, Requires: Temple of Nod, Advanced laser defense
