=== Command & Conquer Strategy Guide for OpenRA ===

This guide explains the basic mechanics and units for the C&C mod in OpenRA. 
GDI and Nod share most basic units with faction-specific advanced units.

== BUILD ORDER REQUIREMENTS ==

You MUST follow this exact order at game start:
1. Deploy your MCV (Mobile Construction Vehicle) to create your Construction Yard
2. Build a Power Plant (NUKE) - Cost: $500, Provides: 100 power
3. Build a Refinery (PROC) - Cost: $1500, Requires: Power, Includes: 1 free Harvester
4. Build Barracks (PYLE/HAND) - Cost: $500, Requires: Power
5. Build War Factory/Airfield - Cost: $2000, Requires: Refinery

YOU CANNOT BUILD A REFINERY WITHOUT POWER!
YOU CANNOT BUILD HARVESTERS WITHOUT A WAR FACTORY OR AIRFIELD!

== ECONOMY ==

- Tiberium is your only resource, appearing as green or blue crystals on the map
- Harvesters automatically collect Tiberium and deliver to Refineries
- Each Refinery comes with 1 free Harvester when built
- Additional Harvesters cost $1100 and require War Factory/Airfield
- Build multiple Refineries on different Tiberium fields for faster income
- Tiberium Storage Silos ($100) increase storage capacity

== KEY STRUCTURES ==

=== Basic Buildings ===
- Construction Yard (FACT): Your command center, build from deployed MCV
- Power Plant (NUKE): $500, provides 100 power
- Advanced Power Plant (NUK2): $800, provides 200 power (requires tech center)
- Refinery (PROC): $1500, processes Tiberium, includes 1 Harvester
- Barracks (GDI: PYLE, Nod: HAND): $500, trains infantry
- War Factory (GDI: WEAP): $2000, builds vehicles
- Airfield (Nod: AFLD): $2000, builds vehicles via airdrop

=== Defensive Structures ===
- Guard Tower (GDI): Anti-infantry defense
- Turret (Nod): Anti-vehicle defense
- SAM Site: Anti-air defense
- Obelisk of Light (Nod): Advanced laser defense

=== Advanced Buildings ===
- Communications Center: Provides radar minimap
- Temple of Nod (TMPL): $2000, Nod tech center, enables Nuclear Missile
- Advanced Communications Center (EYE): GDI tech center, enables Ion Cannon
- Helipad (HPAD): Builds and rearms aircraft

== INFANTRY UNITS ==

- Minigunner (E1): $100, basic anti-infantry
- Grenadier (E2): $160, anti-infantry/building (GDI)
- Rocket Soldier (E3): $300, anti-tank/air
- Flamethrower (E4): $200, anti-infantry/building (Nod)
- Chem Warrior (E5): $300, advanced anti-infantry (Nod)
- Engineer (E6): $500, captures buildings, repairs bridges
- Commando (RMBO): $1500, sniper + C4 demolitions (GDI)

== VEHICLES ==

=== Support ===
- Harvester (HARV): $1100, collects Tiberium (first one free with Refinery)
- Mobile Construction Vehicle (MCV): $3000, deploys into Construction Yard
- APC: $600, transports 5 infantry (GDI)
- Transport Helicopter (TRAN): $750, air transport

=== Combat Vehicles ===
- Nod Buggy (BGGY): $300, fast scout with machine gun
- Recon Bike (BIKE): $500, fast rocket scout
- Humvee (JEEP): $400, light vehicle (GDI)
- Light Tank (LTNK): $750, basic tank (Nod)
- Medium Tank (MTNK): $900, main battle tank (GDI)
- Flame Tank (FTNK): $600, anti-infantry (Nod)
- Artillery (ARTY): $600, long-range support (Nod)
- Mammoth Tank (HTNK): $1800, heavy dual-cannon tank (GDI)
- Stealth Tank (STNK): $900, cloaked missile tank (Nod)
- Rocket Launcher (MSAM): $900, mobile SAM (GDI)
- Mobile Rocket Launch System (MLRS): $600, anti-air (Nod)

== AIRCRAFT ==

- Orca (GDI): $1200, VTOL gunship with missiles
- Apache (HELI): $1200, attack helicopter (Nod)
- Chinook (TRAN): $750, transport helicopter
- A-10 Warthog: Support power aircraft
- C-17: Transport plane for vehicle delivery (NOT FOR SCOUTING!)

== STRATEGY TIPS ==

1. Economy First: Build order is critical - Power → Refinery → Production
2. Multiple Refineries: Better than multiple Harvesters early game
3. Scout with cheap units: Use Minigunners or Buggies, NOT transports
4. Combined Arms: Mix infantry, vehicles, and air units
5. Engineers: Can capture enemy buildings for tech stealing
6. Power Management: Buildings operate slowly without sufficient power
7. Base Defense: Guard Towers vs infantry, Turrets vs vehicles
8. Superweapons: Ion Cannon (GDI) and Nuclear Missile (Nod) require tech centers

== COMMON MISTAKES ==

- Trying to build Harvesters before War Factory/Airfield
- Using C-17 transport planes for scouting (they're for cargo!)
- Building Harvesters instead of additional Refineries early
- Forgetting Power Plants before other structures
- Not spreading Refineries to different Tiberium fields

Remember: The Temple is "Temple of Nod" not "Temple of Ra"!
